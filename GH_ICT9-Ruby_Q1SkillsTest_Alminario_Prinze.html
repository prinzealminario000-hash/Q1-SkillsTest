About Our Club

Welcome to the OBMC Rubics Club! We are a community of passionate students who love exploring the world of robotics, programming, and engineering. Our club provides a platform for students to learn, innovate, and collaborate on exciting robotics projects.

Whether you're a beginner or an experienced coder, our club offers opportunities for everyone to grow their skills and work on challenging projects. We participate in competitions, host workshops, and organize fun events throughout the year.

Join us to be part of a team that builds everything from line-following robots to advanced autonomous systems!
